Brent Faiyaz is an American singer, songwriter, and producer known for his unique sound at the crossroads of alternative R&B, neo-soul, hip-hop, and trap. His music, both minimalist and atmospheric, blends sensuality, melancholy, and introspection, exploring themes such as love, loneliness, and fame. Through standout projects like Sonder Son(2017) and WASTELAND(2022), Brent has established himself as one of the most distinctive voices in modern R&B, constantly playing with contrasts between softness and disillusionment. As a fully independent artist, he founded his own label, Lost Kids, to preserve his creative freedom. True to himself, he has collaborated with major artists such as Drake, Tyler, The Creator, and The Neptunes, while remaining in full control of his sound and emotional vision.
